Codewords


How to Start a Game:

Type in a room name and click 'Create a Room' to create a new room

How to Join a Game:

To join a room, click 'Find Available Rooms' and click on a room name.

How to Play Codewords:

Players split up into teams of two. You need four players to begin a game. Each team has one player as their codemaster. Both codemasters can see the colored 'codemaster' view. The other players are 'guessers' and they take turns guessing words that may match the clue their codemaster provides.
